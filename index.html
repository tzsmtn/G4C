PITCH

Outgrow Hunger is a survival adventure game where the player starts weak and starving in a harsh world. You explore, gather food, and learn new skills to survive. As you grow stronger, hunger stops controlling you and becomes something you overcome.

The goal is not just survival. The goal is growth. Players slowly transform from someone desperate for food into someone capable of helping others survive.

THEME

The theme of the game is resilience and growth. Hunger represents struggle in life. Through exploration, learning, and persistence, the player shows that hardships can be overcome.

MY WHY

I made this game to show how people can grow past their struggles. Everyone faces some kind of hunger, whether it is for food, success, or purpose. This game is about becoming stronger and not letting hardship define who you are.
