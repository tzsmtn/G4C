PITCH

End.co is a survival adventure game where you start weak and starving in a harsh world. Explore, scavenge for food, and unlock new skills just to survive another day.

The goal it's growth. Transform from a desperate scavenger into someone strong enough to help others survive too.

THEME

The theme is resilience and growth. Hunger represents the struggles we all face in life.

Through exploration, learning, and persistence, the player shows that hardship isn't a dead end — it's a starting point for something greater.

MY WHY

I made this game because everyone faces some kind of hunger — for food, success, or purpose.

Outgrow Hunger is about proving that struggles don't define you. What matters is the choice to keep going and rise above.
