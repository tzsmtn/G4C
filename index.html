end.co WHY THEME PITCH

end.co

Pitch

end.co is a strategy game where you build a global food network to fight hunger. Instead of spreading a disease like in Plague Inc, you spread food through farms, logistics, and community programs.

Manage resources, survive global crises, and build a food system strong enough to keep people fed.

Theme

The game focuses on resilience and systems thinking. Feeding people is not just about growing food. It is about logistics, economics, and cooperation.

Every decision changes the global system and shows how connected the food supply really is.

Why

I made this game to show how complicated global hunger is. Turning the food system into a strategy game lets players see how policies, markets, and crises affect whether people eat.

The goal is to make players think about real solutions to a real world problem.
